## The Four Boundaries

- **Ingest**: validate shape, reject poison early, and capture trace context.
- **Process**: keep work small, make retries safe, and treat timeouts as normal.
- **Persist**: use idempotency keys and durable writes with clear ownership.
- **Publish**: emit outcomes that consumers can reason about (and re-play).

## Idempotency: The Superpower

Assume duplicates. Assume retries. Assume replays. If your design tolerates those, the rest becomes a performance problem you can measure and tune.

<Callout title="Rule of thumb" variant="teal">
  Make the correct thing easy to retry, and the incorrect thing hard to trigger.
</Callout>
