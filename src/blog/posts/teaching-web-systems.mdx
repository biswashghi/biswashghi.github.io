## Three Demos That Help

- DNS as a series of caches with expiration, not a magic phonebook.
- CSR vs SSR as a latency tradeoff, not a religious war.
- REST APIs as contracts that evolve, not endpoints you copy/paste.

<Callout title="Small advice" variant="sand">
  If students can predict the next failure mode, they have learned something durable.
</Callout>
