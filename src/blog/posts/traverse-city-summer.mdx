After my most recent 2 day trip to Traverse City, touring wine country, driving down M-22 and again seeing the breathtaking vistas of Western Michigan, I'm already planning my next trip there.

---

In the next couple months, I want to move to NYC. Not due to some long held dream of living it up in the Big Apple, but more as a goal and drive for myself to do more.
I've enjoyed a stable life of 6 years post college here in Michigan with family. I've made some great friends, traveled a lot with family, and have had a very enjoyable 6 years.

However, in this time here in Michigan, I've gotten comfortable. This comfort has manifested in a lack of energy to try for something harder and push myself to do better.

I never intended this trip to Traverse City to be anything special or have any relation to this goal of mine but after this 3 day trip, I think I've found some threads that connect these two in ways I never thought before.
